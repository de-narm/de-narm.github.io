The Club Behind the Software Explorama is developed and maintained by Explorama e.V., a registered non-profit organization (eingetragener Verein) dedicated to creating accessible data analytics tools. As a community-driven club, we bring together developers, data enthusiasts, and subject matter experts who share a common vision.Our club operates on the principles of open source collaboration, transparency, and inclusivity. We believe in the power of community-driven development and the importance of making technology accessible to all.As a registered German non-profit organization (e.V.), we are committed to serving the public interest by providing free, open-source software that empowers people to work with data effectively.
